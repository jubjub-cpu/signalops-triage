SignalOps is a local-first, synthetic-data product demo for ATM, kiosk, locker, and equipment-maintenance teams. It shows where AI can help coordinators classify incidents, spot repeated-failure cues, score urgency, and draft technician actions while leaving dispatch approval to a human.

Business Problem

Operations teams lose time when field reports arrive as inconsistent notes, recurrence patterns are easy to miss, and dispatch actions are not clearly justified.

Target User

Field-service coordinators, ATM/kiosk operations leads, and maintenance teams reviewing incidents before assigning a technician.

AI Role

Classify incident type, score severity, identify repeated-failure language, and draft an action plan in deterministic demo mode.

Human Oversight

No recommendation is final until a coordinator approves or sends it back for more context.

Why this would matter to a business

SignalOps demonstrates a practical middle ground: AI helps structure the incident review, but the team keeps accountability for dispatch, escalation, and customer-facing action. The demo uses fictional equipment records only.
